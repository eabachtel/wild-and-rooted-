About Us

Welcome to Wild & Rooted!

At Wild & Rooted, we’re passionate about connecting people with nature, one plant at a time. We specialize in hand-selected, rooted plant clippings that bring a piece of the outdoors into your space, promoting calm and inspiration through greenery.

Our Mission

To cultivate a love for plants by offering carefully rooted clippings that thrive in your home, bringing beauty, tranquility, and sustainability to your everyday environment.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone, from plant enthusiasts to beginners, has access to beautiful and easy-to-care-for plants, enhancing homes with vibrant greenery.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Each clipping is rooted in an environmentally friendly process, using minimal resources.
  • Quality: We prioritize healthy, strong clippings, ensuring each plant is ready to thrive in its new home.
  • Accessibility: Our goal is to make indoor plants approachable and manageable for everyone, regardless of experience level.

Care Tips & Plant Info

Monstera Deliciosa

Monstera thrives in indirect light and prefers slightly moist soil. Ideal in humid spaces.

Pothos

Prefers moderate light but adapts well to low light. Water when the top inch of soil feels dry.

Snake Plant

Best in low to bright indirect light. Water sparingly; allow soil to dry completely between watering.
