About Wild & Rooted

Hi, I’m Liz, the founder of Wild & Rooted! I started this business to share my love for plants and help others bring nature’s beauty into their homes.

Wild & Rooted was born out of a passion for nurturing life and sharing nature’s gifts with others. Each plant we offer is carefully grown with love and respect, embodying our mission to make plant care accessible to all.
