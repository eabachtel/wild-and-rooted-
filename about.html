About Wild & Rooted

Hi, I’m Liz, and I started Wild & Rooted from my love of plants and the joy they bring to life.

I believe that plants can transform any space, making it feel alive, calm, and full of potential.

Wild & Rooted is a small, heart-driven business, and each plant cutting I prepare is grown and nurtured with love.

It’s my mission to make plant care simple, rewarding, and accessible for everyone.

Thank you for letting me be a part of your green journey, and I hope Wild & Rooted brings a touch of nature’s peace to your home.
