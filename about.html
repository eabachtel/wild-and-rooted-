About Wild & Rooted

Hello! I'm Liz, the founder of Wild & Rooted. As a lifelong plant enthusiast and IT technician by day, I started this business with a mission to make plant care accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable for everyone.

Wild & Rooted is dedicated to offering high-quality plant clippings along with easy-to-follow care tips. Each plant is selected for its unique beauty and ability to thrive indoors, helping to bring the calming, vibrant presence of nature into your home.

Thank you for joining me on this green journey, and I hope our plants help you create a peaceful, rooted space of your own.
